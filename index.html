Mission Statement

Welcome to the website for coordinating observations of the recently discovered interstellar object (ISO) 3I-ATLAS! Our intention is to provide a central hub for information regarding this ISO. We encourage and facilitate the acquisition of observations and promote collaborations between researchers.

What makes it interstellar? 3I-ATLAS has a hyperbolic orbit (eccentricity > 1), meaning it came from outside our solar system and will eventually leave.
